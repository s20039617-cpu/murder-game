=== NEW PHASE SYSTEM IMPLEMENTED ===

PHASE CYCLE (Repeating):
1. Day Phase 1 (30 seconds)
   - Two random players selected for private chat
   - Other players cannot see/interact
   - Selected players receive: day1Players array in phase message

2. Day Phase 2 (30 seconds)
   - Full group discussion
   - All players can see each other
   - Voting preparation begins

3. Voting Phase (20 seconds)
   - Players vote to eliminate someone
   - Votes are tallied
   - Elimination happens

4. Night Phase (20 seconds)
   - Murder/Seer/Guard take actions
   - Private night actions processed
   
Then cycle repeats back to Day Phase 1

TOTAL CYCLE TIME: 30 + 30 + 20 + 20 = 100 seconds
